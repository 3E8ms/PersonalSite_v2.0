About me

I am now a 4th year student of University of Waterloo. Made this website with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and Node.js. Got proficient use of C, C++, python,VB and scheme during my course + homework. Learnt working knowledge of VBA and Java during previous work terms.

This web is more about myself instead of a code portfolio, please check my code work in GitHub.

I love all art-related stuff. I take photos, paint, and make designs. Artworks can be checked here in Lofter and Instagram. I started Lofter in 2015 and updated all my drawings there. Instagram is for sharing daily life and some radom photos.

I have a 1-yr-old cat named Peace Chen, a blue lynx bicolor cat, although he is not blue, lynx or bicolor:P. He is really a soft + gentle cat. Photos of Peace can also be checked in Instagram.
